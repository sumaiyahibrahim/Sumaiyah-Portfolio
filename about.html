About Me

Hey there! 👋 I’m Sumaiyah, a web developer who vibes with both pixels and Python. I craft responsive apps, experiment with AI/ML, and turn wild ideas into clean, functional code.

My toolbox? React for sleek UIs, LangChain & LLMs for AI magic, and Python for data-driven experiments. I love that sweet spot where design meets logic.

I’m all about learning by building — breaking things, fixing them smarter, and pushing ideas into reality. Whether it’s web apps, AI tools, or fun prototypes, I’m here to make tech feel smooth, smart, and human.

This site? More than just a portfolio — it’s my sandbox, my progress log, and sometimes my playground for experiments. Coffee ☕ may or may not be involved.
